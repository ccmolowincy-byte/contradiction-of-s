The Contradiction of S

The Contradiction of S explores how people live with, adapt to, and negotiate scoliosis.

Developed through interviews, research, and lived experience, the project translates stories, coping strategies, and embodied knowledge into physical artefacts and a growing digital archive.

By activating the AR experience, you will encounter a collective garden of movements surrounding the sculptures. As more people participate, each gesture becomes part of a larger landscape of shared experience.

You are invited to contribute a movement and become part of this evolving archive.
